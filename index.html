JOIN THE TEAM

At Artbeat, we’re constantly striving to build a troupe of first-principle thinkers and zealous risk-takers. We believe in adding the driven, bright, young and energetic creative minds who should use every given opportunity to showcase their real selves and take Artbeat further to new heights. They should desire to help us push the envelope and help us bring a change which would bind the viewers with us and take challenges to learn something new in every project. Artbeat is all about viewers and we want best and unique for them and our team.


Our Culture:

  • Give and ask for feedback proactively.
  • Convince or be convinced -No HiPPO effect.
  • Be transparent, share information and knowledge at every step.
  • Work as a sports team - “Talent wins games, but teamwork and intelligence win championships."
  • Over-communicate and ask for guidance whenever and wherever needed.
  • Under-promise and Over-deliver.
  • We have offices across Gaya, Bihar and New Delhi.
  • Our workspaces are as vibrant as the minds of those who work with us!
  • Think you’ve got what it takes?
